SEO Pricing FAQs

Still have questions about our plans? Reach out and we'll help you find the perfect fit for your business.

Get In Touch!
How much does SEO cost per month?
Our SEO packages start at $500/month for the Basic plan and go up to $1,800/month for the Premium plan. Each plan is tailored to different business sizes and goals.
What's included in the Gold plan?
The Gold plan includes up to 60 keyword targets, 60 backlinks/month, 25 optimized pages, guest posting, AI competitor tracking, full AI/LLM schema, voice search optimization, and dedicated support via email, phone, and chat.
How long before I see SEO results?
SEO is a long-term investment. Most clients see meaningful improvements within 3-6 months, with significant ROI by month 6-12. AI-optimized strategies often show faster results in AI-driven search platforms.
What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?
GEO optimizes your content to be cited and recommended by AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. It's included in our Gold and Premium plans.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. We'll adjust your strategy and deliverables seamlessly to match your new tier.
Do you offer custom packages?
Yes! If none of our standard plans fit your needs, we can create a custom package tailored to your specific business goals and budget. Contact us to discuss your requirements.
